AUTOMOTIVE PROGRESSIVE DIE PIECE PART

At ViTO Technologies we offer solutions that deliver efficient, cost-effective, progressive dies to our customers.

The part highlighted here represent a cross section of our capabilities. Working with HSLA and multi-phase materials.

Designed for use within a commercial truck door latch, this component is comprised of .120” cold rolled carbon steel. Assigned to a 200-ton coil fed press, features include 4” overall length, shaved pierce openings, and critical form tolerances. ViTO Technologies strives to be your go to shop for critical projects.
